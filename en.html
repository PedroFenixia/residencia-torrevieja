Frequently Asked Questions

We answer your questions

How much does a luxury senior residence in Torrevieja cost?

At Mar Bella Senior Residence, fees depend on the type of room (single or double) and the additional services your loved one may need. Contact us on 965 710 828 for a personalised quote with no obligation.

What services are included in the stay?

All our residents receive: 24-hour medical and nursing care, full board with in-house kitchen and personalised home-cooked menus, daily social and cultural activities, laundry and cleaning service, and access to all common areas including the terrace with sea views.

Do you offer physiotherapy and rehabilitation?

Yes, we have physiotherapists specialising in geriatrics who provide personalised rehabilitation programmes, functional maintenance exercises, fall prevention and daily adapted gymnastics sessions for each resident.

Can I visit the residence before making a decision?

Absolutely. We offer no-obligation visits every day from 10:00 to 20:00. You can arrange your visit by calling 965 710 828 or 677 919 951, or by sending a WhatsApp message.

Do you accept residents with Alzheimer's or dementia?

Yes, we specialise in memory care. We have cognitive stimulation programmes, specialised psychological support and staff trained in caring for people with cognitive impairment.

Where exactly is the residence located?

We are at Avda. Alfredo Nobel, 8, 03183 Torrevieja, Alicante, Spain. We are the only senior residence by the sea in Torrevieja, just 10 minutes from the town centre and 25 minutes from Alicante-Elche Airport.

What types of rooms do you have?

We have single and double rooms, all bright and airy, with adapted bathroom, TV, telephone and many with a private terrace and Mediterranean sea views. Following the recent renovation, all rooms feature completely updated facilities.

What is the climate like in Torrevieja?

Torrevieja enjoys a privileged climate with 320 days of sunshine a year and an average temperature of 18°C. The WHO has recognised it as one of the cities with the best climate in the world, ideal for the health of elderly people.
